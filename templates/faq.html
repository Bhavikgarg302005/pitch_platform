General Information

What is E-shark Tank?
E-shark Tank is a platform where individuals can explore and pitch innovative ideas to investors and other participants.
How can I register as a pitcher?
You can register as a pitcher by visiting change Portfolio to pitcher option.
How Can I view all the pitches?
You can explore the pitches by clicking on the view all option on recent pitches.

Account and Profile

How can I view my profile?
You can view your profile information by navigating to your profile page, where you can see your details, including your name, email etc..
What should I do if I forget my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.
